## Codebase Patterns (critical for agents)
- All scene content built in _ready(), not in .tscn editor
- Inner classes: class _Foo extends Node2D (inside parent .gd file)
- Enemy signals: shoot_at(pos: Vector2), use add_child + queue_free manually
- Collision: manual distance checks in Game._process(), not Area2D signals
- Audio: _make_beep(freq, duration, player_index) in AudioSystem.gd
- save/load: SaveSystem.load_game() returns Dictionary, SaveSystem.save_game(state)
- Constants: GameData.GAME_WIDTH = 1920, GameData.GAME_HEIGHT = 1080
- Enemy base: extends Area2D, _draw_shape() override, hit(damage) method
